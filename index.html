12 · Risks (Honest)
We see three risks.
We have answers.

Dream interpretation is subjective

True. But the product doesn't claim to "interpret" — it visualizes. The user sees their dream as video. Subjectivity becomes a feature: every result is unique.

AI generation cost

Currently ~$0.10–0.50 per dream video. Falling 50%+ per year. At scale, cost approaches zero. Unit economics improve with every user.

Retention / habit formation

Dreams happen automatically every night — no motivation needed. The "watch your dream" reward is immediate. Natural daily loop, not forced engagement.

The upside asymmetry: if this works, we own an entirely new media category with daily engagement, viral content, and a data moat that compounds forever. The downside is a failed app. The upside is a new platform.
